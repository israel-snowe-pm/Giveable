Feed people.
Not just wallets.

A giving platform designed for Winnipeg.

Giveable is a behavioral experiment. We're testing whether showing donors their impact in meals funded — rather than dollars — changes how often young Winnipeggers give, and whether they come back.

Most hunger charities in this city have almost no regular donors under 35. Not because young people don't care — because the experience of giving was never designed for them. Giveable is built to change that. One meal counter at a time.

We're currently in development, building toward a free 60-day pilot with 2–3 Winnipeg hunger organizations. If you run one of them — or know someone who does — we'd genuinely like to talk.
